About My Meta Foundation

Launch pad for NFT projects created by a group of global artists

Strong communities within the NFT space, but also to give back by supporting as many global charities as possible

  • The MyMetaFoundation was created as a launch pad for NFT projects created by a group of global artists with the aim of not only creating awesome collectables and building strong communities within the NFT space, but also to give back by supporting as many global charities as possible.
  • We have colaborated with The Giving Block, an organization whose partnerships with 1000+ pro-crypto charitable foundations enables donations to be made directly to the partner charities or The Giving Block’s own Crypto Adoption Fund in a variety of cyrptocurrencies.

How it works

A Concrete Help For A Better & kind World

To utilise the blockchain to align the future of giving with the charities of the present by creating amazing art and outstanding supportive communities.

  • As an owner of a MyMetaFoundation NFT you will benefit from a host of ongoing benefits, from free NFTs from future collections and Whitelist entries to free or discounted access to our live online charitable events, ranging from Metaverse comedy shows and live interviews and discusions, to fully immersive music concerts with your favourite artists.
  • In order to take advantage of the openess and agility of the blockchain the smart contract for each NFT Collection are coded to transfer a percentage of all initial sales and future royalities directly to the chosen charity’s cyrpto wallet via The Giving Block platform. This means that for the first time ever, giving is now imutable and everlasting.
