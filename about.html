How It works

The MyMetaFoundation was created as a launch pad for NFT projects created by a group of global artists with the aim of not only creating awesome collectables and building strong communities within the NFT space, but also to give back by supporting as many global charities as possible.


We have colaborated with The Giving Block, an organization whose partnerships with 1000+ pro-crypto charitable foundations enables donations to be made directly to the partner charities or The Giving Block’s own Crypto Adoption Fund in a variety of cyrptocurrencies.
